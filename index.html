1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  2. You are either the play icon (Green) or the Stop icon (red). Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  3. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. The winners' score will be incremented by 1
  4. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
  5. You win by having 5 points
